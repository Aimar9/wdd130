History

Rafting began in the 19th century as a means of transport, with the first known attempt in 1811 and the first modern raft in the 1840s, but it became popular as a sport and commercial activity in the United States in the 1950s and 1960s. Its origins lie in military and rescue rafts, evolving into tourist activities and international competitions, with an International Rafting Federation (FIC) promoting its safe practice and global development.
