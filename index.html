Cuzco

Cusco is a historic city in southeastern Peru and the former capital of the Inca Empire. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, impressive archaeological sites, and beautiful colonial architecture. Today, Cusco is one of the country's most popular tourist destinations and serves as the main gateway to Machu Picchu.
